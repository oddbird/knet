What we offer

Individualization & differentiation

Introduce teachers to digital tools that generate data on each student so that teachers can be more responsive to student needs and can easily personalize lessons for all students.

Khan Academy Integration

Work with schools and teachers to implement Khan Academy in classrooms efficiently and for maximum results. Preliminary data shows that when adopted effectively and in its entirety, students’ understanding can improve so much that they work up to three years ahead of their grade level.

The “Flipped Classroom”

Help teachers to transfer the classroom lecture to the student’s home after class, freeing up classroom time for problem solving and project creation. This allows teachers to increase the time they can spend 1-on-1 and in small groups with students.
