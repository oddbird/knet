What we offer

Personalized Learning

Introduce teachers to digital tools that generate data on each student so that teachers can be more responsive to student needs and can easily differentiate & individualize lessons for all students.

Khan Academy Integration

Work with schools and teachers to implement Khan Academy in classrooms efficiently and for maximum results. When adopted effectively, students’ understanding can improve up to three years ahead of their grade level.

The “Flipped Classroom”

Help teachers transfer the classroom lecture to the student’s home after class, freeing up classroom time for problem solving, project creation, and 1-on-1 time with students and small groups.

Who We Are

Peter Kuperman

Peter has a background in asset management. He has invested over 1,200 volunteer hours for Khan Academy: mapping content to curriculum, leading a team of 14 to build the teacher’s resource section, and coding exercises for the knowledge map. His infectious enthusiasm inspired Sal Khan to make his first ever trip to Canada for meetings with business & education leaders.

Emmanuelle Deaton

Emmanuelle is a veteran teacher with eight years in the classroom. She has worked in multiple school systems with a range of administrative teams. She understands teachers and how to coach them for the 21st century arena. Her focus is on high student engagement & collaboration, skill development, and higher-order thinking.
