Consulting Services

Personalized Learning

Introduce teachers to digital tools that generate data on each student so they can easily differentiate & individualize lessons in response to student needs.

Khan Academy Integration

Work with schools & teachers to incorporate Khan Academy in classrooms. When adopted effectively, students’ understanding has improved up to three years ahead of their grade level.

The “Flipped Classroom”

Help teachers transfer the classroom lecture to the student’s home after class, freeing up classroom time for problem solving, project creation, and 1-on-1 time with students.
